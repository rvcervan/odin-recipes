Soft Boiled Egg

Soft Boiled eggs containing runny yet cooked egg yolk in the center.

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Salt and pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Fill a pot with water and bring it up to a boil.
  2. Place your eggs into the pot of water and leave it for 6 minutes.
  3. After 6 minutes, remove the eggs from the pot and place into ice water to stop the eggs from cooking.
